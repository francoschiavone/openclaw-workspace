## Part 3: Pricing Models That Convert

### What Works in 2025-2026

**Hybrid Pricing Dominates:**
- 91% of SaaS use usage-based or hybrid models
- Hybrid models show stronger revenue performance than flat plans
- Base subscription + usage tiers = predictable revenue + upside

### Proven Pricing Patterns

| Model | Best For | Example |
|-------|----------|---------|
| **Base + Credits** | AI tools with variable compute | $29/mo + $0.005/generation |
| **Tiered by Volume** | Document processing | $49/100 docs, $99/500 docs |
| **Outcome-Based** | Support/automation | $0.99 per resolved ticket (Intercom) |
| **Per-Seat + Usage** | Team tools | $30/user + credits for AI calls |
| **Workflow-Based** | Task automation | $X per completed task |

### Key Pricing Insights

1. **Annual plans cut churn by 30%** and increase LTV by 27%
2. **14-day free trials** convert better than freemium for B2B
3. **$79/month is painkiller pricing** - $19/month is vitamin pricing
4. **Outcome-based pricing** ($0.99/resolution) creates perfect value alignment
5. **Hide tokens from customers** - anchor pricing on workflows/outcomes

| Early Traction | $100-$500 | Basic hosting + AI API calls |

### AI API Costs (2026)
- **GPT-4o:** $2.50/$10 per million tokens (input/output)
- **Claude 3.5 Sonnet:** $3/$15 per million tokens
- **Gemini Pro:** $1.25/$5 per million tokens
- **Open-source (Llama 3.1):** ~$0.10-$0.50 per million tokens (self-hosted)

### Cost Optimization Strategies

1. **Use smaller models** for simple tasks (classification, extraction)
2. **Cache embeddings** for RAG - don't re-embed unchanged documents
3. **Batch processing** instead of real-time where possible
4. **Use AI internally first** before adding customer-facing features
5. **Start with hosted APIs**, optimize to self-hosted only at scale

## Part 7: Emerging Trends 2026

### The Agent Leap
- AI orchestrates complex, end-to-end workflows semi-autonomously
- **Agent-as-a-Service (AaaS)** models emerging
- Multi-agent orchestration becoming mainstream
- Human-in-the-loop systems winning over fully autonomous

### Key 2026 Trends

1. **Vertical SaaS dominates** - Horizontal SaaS saturated
2. **AI Agents replace simple chatbots** - Workflow execution, not just chat
3. **Build vs. Buy inflection** - Custom AI apps becoming easier than buying SaaS
4. **Governance-by-design** - Not an afterthought
5. **Outcome-based pricing** - Standard for agent products

### Unexplored / Low-Competition Areas

1. **Industry-specific AI agents** - Insurance claims, legal research, medical coding
2. **Voice AI for blue-collar industries** - After-hours answering for trades
3. **Compliance automation** - Regulated industries drowning in paperwork
4. **AI for non-profits/government** - Underserved, budget available
5. **Multilingual AI tools** - Hispanic market in US, LATAM expansion
